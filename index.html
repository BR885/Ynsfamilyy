About YNS Family

We are more than just a dance studio - we're a family united by our love for movement, rhythm, and artistic expression.

Our Story

Founded by co-founders Gheko Hunter and Akatuski Yamato Shinoda, YNS Family was born from a shared vision to create a welcoming space for dancers of all levels. What started as a dream has grown into a vibrant community where creativity flourishes and dreams take flight.

Our experienced instructors, led by our passionate founders, bring expertise, innovation, and individual attention to every class, ensuring that each student receives the guidance they need to reach their full potential in Hip-Hop and Drill dance.
